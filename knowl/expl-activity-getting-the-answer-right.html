Exploration 7.1. Which Model(s) to Use?

So far, we have worked with the SI, SIR, and SEIR models. We have shown how to include vaccines in a model. And for those who completed the last exercise of For Further ThoughtÂ 6.3, we showed how to include vaccines along with their efficacy or effectiveness. Now, think through choosing from these models for different scenarios.

(a)

Which model(s) would you choose to work with if you wanted to include a term such as \((1-p)\) to indicate vaccination? Why?

(b)

Which model(s) would allow you to change the length of time someone is Infectious, perhaps to test the outcome of a medication that reduces the length of time a person can infect others? Why does your choice of model(s) make sense?

(c)

Which model(s) would you use if you had an Infectious compartment in which individuals would always stay Infectious? Why?

(d)

Which model(s) would allow you to change the time scale from days to weeks? How would you know this is possible?
