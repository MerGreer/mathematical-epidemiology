After completing Activity 4.5, we recognize that in our SIR model, the units of \(\beta\) are not \(1/\mbox{time}\text{.}\) Therefore \(\beta\) is not a rate, and we cannot estimate the value of \(\beta\) similarly to how we estimate the value of \(\gamma\text{.}\) Indeed, estimating the value of \(\beta\) will require different methods. In Chapter 3 we discuss estimating \(\beta\) visually by comparing our model with data. In later chapters, we discuss other approaches to estimating \(\beta\text{.}\)
