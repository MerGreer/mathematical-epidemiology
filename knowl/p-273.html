If \(\gamma =1\text{,}\) then all members of compartment I, that is, \(100\%\) of the members, leave I at each time step. If \(\gamma =0.5\text{,}\) then half the members, that is, \(50\%\text{,}\) leave I at each time step. And if \(\gamma =0.25\text{,}\) then one-quarter of the members, that is, \(25\%\text{,}\) leave I at each time step.
