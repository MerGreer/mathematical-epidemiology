Decide on a model that you would use for an influenza outbreak on the campus you are imagining. Write out your reasons for the modeling decisions you make. Ideas to consider include: which compartmental model? How many people are involved, and what are the initial numbers of people in each compartment? What are the parameter values? What ranges of values may be possible for initial values and parameter values? There may be yet other aspects of your model to describe and explain.
