If \(\gamma =1\text{,}\) then all members of compartment I leave at each time step, so a typical individual stays in I for one time step. If \(\gamma =0.5\text{,}\) then half the members of I leave at each time step, so a typical individual stays in I for approximately two time steps. And if \(\gamma =0.25\text{,}\) then one-quarter of the members of I leave at each time step, so a typical individual stays in I for approximately four time steps.
