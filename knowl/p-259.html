When zombies die, they leave the Infectious compartment and can no longer infect the Susceptibles. It is typical to represent these dynamics by introducing a Removed (R) compartment for the individuals who were zombies but have then become inactive. Our model then includes S, I, and R compartments, so we call it an SIR model. 2  A compartmental diagram representing the dynamics of this SIR model appears in Figure 4.3.
