An SEIR model is a compartmental model having four compartments, labeled S, E, I, and R. Most often in this text, S stands for Susceptible, E stands for Exposed-and-incubating, I stands for Infectious, and R stands for Removed, though the compartments can have other interpretations. The letters S, E, I, and R are each pronounced when we say “SEIR model”: “Ess” “Eee” “Eye” “Arr” model. (Chapter 6)
