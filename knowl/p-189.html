Regarding \(\beta\text{,}\) we are not seeking a mathematically precise “best fit”. Instead, we are (1) visually identifying what a good fit may look like, and (2) describing the features of a good fit, from a visual perspective. The features may be somewhat subjective: there is not likely to be a perfect fit, and different readers may have different priorities regarding how a good fit “should” look.
